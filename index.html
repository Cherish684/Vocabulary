Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn points?

You earn 5 points each time you learn a new word. You can also earn additional points by completing achievements and reaching new levels.

How do I unlock new levels?

You need to collect 100 points to unlock the Intermediate level and 200 points to unlock the Advanced level.

Can I use VocabBoost offline?

VocabBoost stores your learned words locally, so you can review your saved words offline. However, searching for new words requires an internet connection.

How can I report an incorrect definition?

You can report incorrect definitions by clicking the "Feedback" tab and submitting your correction through the feedback form. Our team will review and update it accordingly.

Are there any subscription plans available?

VocabBoost offers both free and premium subscription plans. The free plan provides access to basic features, while the premium plan includes advanced features like personalized learning paths and ad-free experience.

What is the AI Agent section?

The AI Agent section features two powerful tools: Word Net (discover synonyms, antonyms, and rhyming words) and Word Clue (test your knowledge with interactive quizzes). Simply enter a word and let our AI agents help you learn!
